Booking Configuration

Day Count specifies how many days are displayed on your booking calendar. The default is 2 weeks, allowing clients to book up to 2 weeks out.

Segment Time specifies how your time blocks are shown on your calendar. For example, if you specify 60 minutes as your Segment Time, your booking calendar will display hourly time slots (e.g. 8:00am, 9:00am, 10:00am, etc). If you specify 15 for Segment Time, then times would display as 8:00am, 8:15am, 8:30am, 8:45am, etc.
To let segment time default to service time, specify zero for the segment time.

Service Times

Service times enable you to customize how much time should be blocked out to complete a service. These fine-tuned service times are then used to block out time on your appointment calendar.

For example, if you set the service time for a men's haircut to 45 minutes, then 45 minutes is blocked on your calendar when someone books a men's haircut.

Optionally you can specify a "process" time, and a "finish" time. This is useful when the service has 3 steps (start, process, and finish) and allows you to overlap clients/services.

For each service, specify a start time, and optionally specify process and finish times.

  • Start time is the amount of time to block on your calendar when a client books a particular service.
  • Process time opens up a gap in your schedule for booking another client (overlapping clients).
  • Finish time blocks out time from your calendar to finish up a client that requires a processing time.

Start, Process, and Finish times are shown in minutes and incremented/decremented in 15-minute steps.
